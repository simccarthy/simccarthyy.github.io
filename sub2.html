The Components to Virtual Reality

Abstract
 This site contains every element that goes into the making of VR headsets. The technology in these devices determine how immersive the experience is. Fully immersive experiences is the most common known term. However, there is three different types of immersion such as semi immersive and non immerive. Starting out with the most essential element of VR, the headset. The headset consists of lenses and screens that create an alternate reality. In order to make these visuals in the VR headset there has to be a frame rate, spatial audio, postition tracking, and field view. These elements go hand in hand with creating an experience outside of reality.
