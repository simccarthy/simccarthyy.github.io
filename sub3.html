 The Physical Impacts of VR
Site Author
 Sandee LaMotte
Abstract
 This site offers multiple health dangers involving VR. Such as cybersickness which can cause the user to have a loss of spatial awareness, nausea, and disorientation.It has been proven that exposure to VR could begin to impact the ability of children to understand the distance of objects. Not only will your eyes be impacts but also the users ability to hear. Listening to high volume sound could damage the users ear drum. Sony suggests that users should use lower volume if they can't hear people around them. Visual content could also affect the users perception of reality. Google Daydream View stated that "If the content is frightening,violent, or anxiety provoking, it can cause your body to react physically".
